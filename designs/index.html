Galatiq · Accounts Payable — Visual Identity Studies

Five ways to look like money.

Five complete app-shell identities for the AP review console — all rendering the same invoices, the same $100,000 fraud case, the same audit trail. So you compare the design, not the data.

Mood · warm & editorial Surface · nav · inbox · review · upload Built · 5 directions, critiqued & refined

Click any study to open it full-screen. Each is a self-contained page with live micro-interactions — hover the inbox rows, the CRITICAL alarm, and the guarded Approve & Pay. The hero of every screen is INV-1003 — Fraudster LLC: an unknown vendor billing $100,000 for an unauthorized item, with wire-transfer urgency. The agents held it; here a human decides.

01 · LEDGEROpen ↗

The Ledger

A 19th-century counting-house day-book, modernized. Cream paper, all-serif, hairline-ruled rows, oldstyle figures.

Signature · the inbox reads as a ruled register with a running held balance; the case file carries a rotated HOLD overstamp and numbered-footnote findings.
02 · ATELIEROpen ↗

The Atelier

A Didone magazine spread. Bone paper, towering high-contrast serif, generous air, one electric vermilion.

Signature · the cover-line — “The $100,000 invoice from a vendor we have never met” — and the amount set as a giant magazine headline.
03 · MANILAOpen ↗

The Manila

A physical filing system, digitized. Warm kraft folders on a desk, humanist type, rubber-stamp status.

Signature · status rendered as slightly-rotated ink-bled rubber stamps; the fraud case is an opened manila file marked HOLD.
04 · ARCHIVEOpen ↗

The Archive

Swiss-editorial structure meets a monospace ledger. Paper white, slab serif headlines, every datum on a visible grid.

Signature · the agent trace and findings as an immaculate monospace ledger, finding codes as mono tags, amber the system accent.
05 · DUSKOpen ↗

The Dusk

Warm editorial, after dark — done right. Candlelit espresso, cream serif, honey-gold glow. No cold dev-tool blue.

Signature · the CRITICAL fraud row smoulders like an ember against the dark; gold hairlines, a luxurious-but-serious night shift.

What these are — standalone design explorations with hardcoded mock data, decoupled from the backend so we can choose an identity fast.
What they're built on — the real domain: the status machine, review categories & alarm levels, findings, and the per-invoice audit trace.
Once you pick a direction, we wire the chosen identity into the live FastAPI app (and add the human-decision endpoint the review screen needs).
