Galatiq · Accounts Payable — Design Studies, Round 2

Thirteen multi-page identities. One calm AP console.

Each is a real navigable mini-app — Inbox → Review → Upload — rendering the same invoices and the same $100,000 Fraudster LLC case, built under a strict less-is-more mandate. Click any study to open its inbox; the nav and rows work. Hover a card to scroll its preview.
